## Overview

This document provides a **template and guidance** for creating a Knowledge Document to accompany the Ticket Writer Gem. It outlines the information teams should supply so the Gem can generate precise, actionable Jira tickets. The more detail provided, the higher the quality of output.

---

## 1. Company / Client Background

### Company Overview

- What does the company do?
- What industry are they in?
- Who are the primary customers or end-users?

### Digital Product(s)

- What is the company's main digital platform or product?
- What problems does it solve?
- Who can access it (customers, admins, partners, etc.)?
- How is it accessed (mobile app, web app)?
- Any limitations, compliance requirements, or regional variations?
- Breakdown of relevant services/features.

---

## 2. Team / Squad Description

### Squad Name

[Insert Squad / Team name]

### Squad Responsibility

- What part of the platform or product does the squad own?
- What features, services, or systems fall under their scope?
- What areas are **not** their responsibility?

## 3. Relevant Context & Documentation

### Dependencies

- External services, systems, or APIs the squad relies on.

### Constraints

- Compliance or regulatory considerations.
- Security, accessibility, or localisation requirements.

### Supporting Documentation

Links or references to:
- Product Requirement Docs (PRDs)
- API specifications
- Architecture diagrams
- Any other relevant documentation

---

## 4. Glossary of Terms & Acronyms

Include all key acronyms, terms, or shorthand your team uses, along with their definitions.  
Include both product/domain-specific and technical/internal terms.

Recommended format:

| Term / Acronym | Definition |
|----------------|------------|
| SSO | Single Sign-On authentication system |
| PRD | Product Requirement Document |
